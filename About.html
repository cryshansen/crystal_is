About

Crystal Hansen has achieved post secondary education in the traditional art teachings of drawing, painting, sculpting structure in space and photography at university level. Through such training, concept of form, space, color, perspective and function lay the groundwork for application. Gaining an art degree gave way to accomplishment in media. Further education was gained by Crystal in the areas of Graphic and Multimedia Design teachings in both university and college settings. Such programs as Photoshop, Image ready, Dreamweaver, Illustrator, Flash, Powerpoint, QuarkXpres, InDesign, Freehand were taught. This gave way to the opportunity to widen the scope of art through multimedia. A decade of the artistic workings of Crystal Hansen are in these pages. Through 10+ yrs of experience in graphic design and print, artistic work evolved and refined. Primarily working in Photoshop, Illustrator, QuarkXpress, in the early years of Graphic design, Crystal formed extensive knowledge of these programs to enable her to be proficient in her work skill set. In later progression, Flash, dreamweaver, ImageReady and powerpoint became part of the norm for program proficiencies. Thus evolving to a Multimedia level of Design knowledge and experience.

Further education in Web Application Development has produced works utilizing .Net Framework (ADO,ASP,VB,C#) and MS SQL, PHP and MySQL, JAVA and MySQL, as well as experience using HTML/CSS, JavaScript and AJAX. Hands on experience with initial site conception to development of site construction. All sites embody functionality and interactive mobility within the site by utilizing CSS code, modular programming styles and validation testing.

Mission Statement to provide the best DESIGN and FUNCTIONALITY promoting INTUITIVE VISUAL CONNECTION between business and their clients in a computerized world.
